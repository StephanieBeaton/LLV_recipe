My Lasagna

comfort food

This Lasagna is fail safe even for the novice cook

Gather the ingredients

12 uncooked lasagna noodles
1 lb lean ground beef
1 tsp oregano
2 bunches spinach
1 1/2 lbs ricotta cheese
3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
1/4 cup freshly grated parmesan cheese
2 (32-oz) tomato sauce cans
pinch nutmeg

Make two fillings

Ricotta layer

Cook spinach for 5 minutes in boiling water. Drain spinach and mix well with ricotta cheese and nutmeg.


Tomato sauce layer

Brown ground beef in frying pan.
Mix ground beef with tomato sauce.

Assemble

  • Pour 1/2 cup of tomato sauce in bottom of 13 by 9 inch baking dish.
  • Cover the tomato sauce layer with uncooked lasagna noodles.
  • Cover the lasagna noodle layer with ricotta cheese filling.
  • Cover the ricotta cheese filling with 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese.
  • Start again with tomato sauce layer. Repeat the layers 3 times.
  • Sprinkle the top with parmesan cheese.
